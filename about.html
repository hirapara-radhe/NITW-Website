History

The National Institute of Technology, Warangal (NITW) was established in 1959 as the Regional Engineering College (REC) by then-Prime Minister Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru, making it the first of the 31 NITs in India. It was later renamed National Institute of Technology, Warangal in 2002 and granted Deemed University status. Over more than six decades, NITW has evolved into a premier engineering institution known for its research, innovation, and strong alumni network

Mission

Imparting total quality education to develop innovative, entrepreneurial and ethical future professionals fit for globally competitive environment. Allowing stakeholders to share our reservoir of experience in education and knowledge for mutual enrichment in the field of technical education. Fostering product oriented research for establishing a self-sustaining and wealth creating centre to serve the societal needs.
